Scope

This privacy policy applies to a personal SMS notification service (the “CarMax Inventory Monitor”) operated by the sole proprietor for the purpose of receiving alerts about new vehicle listings from a public used-car retailer.

Information Collected

The service collects no information from end users. The sender and the sole recipient are the same individual (the sole proprietor). The proprietor’s own mobile phone number is configured directly into the service for the purpose of receiving SMS notifications.

Information Shared

No information is shared with any third party for any purpose. No information is sold, rented, or otherwise transferred. Mobile phone numbers and consent data are not shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Retention

Outbound message logs are retained by the underlying SMS provider (Twilio) per its standard retention policy. No personal data is retained by the sole proprietor outside of the configuration file on a privately-owned server.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technology.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will reflect the most recent revision.
