Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026
Contact: isaiah@patientmagnet.com

Summary

This site exists solely to host the privacy policy and terms of service for a personal SMS notification service (the “CarMax Inventory Monitor”) that the operator runs for themselves. The site collects no information from visitors, and the SMS service collects no information from anyone other than the operator.

Information We Collect

None. This site uses no cookies, no analytics, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting, and no server-side logging beyond the standard access logs maintained by the underlying hosting provider (GitHub Pages).

The associated SMS service stores exactly one phone number — the operator’s own — in a configuration file on a privately-owned server. It is used solely as the destination for self-addressed notifications and is never transmitted anywhere else.

Information We Share

None. No data is sold, rented, traded, transferred, or otherwise disclosed to any third party for any purpose, including marketing.

Third-Party Service Providers

SMS messages are transmitted via Twilio under Twilio’s standard data-handling practices. This static site is hosted on GitHub Pages under GitHub’s standard hosting practices. Neither provider receives any personal data beyond what is technically required to deliver the service.

Data Retention

No personal data is retained by the operator outside of the configuration file. Twilio retains message delivery logs per its own retention policy.

Children

The service is not directed at, intended for, or used by anyone under 18.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.
